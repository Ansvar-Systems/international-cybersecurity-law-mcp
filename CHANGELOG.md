# Changelog

All notable changes to this project will be documented in this file.

The format is based on [Keep a Changelog](https://keepachangelog.com/en/1.1.0/),
and this project adheres to [Semantic Versioning](https://semver.org/spec/v2.0.0.html).

## [0.1.0] - 2026-02-28

### Added

- 14 tools covering international cybersecurity treaties, national strategies, and incident reporting
- 432KB database: 13 sources, 287 treaty articles, 54 national cyber strategies, 24 cyber norms, 15 export controls
- Budapest Convention on Cybercrime (2001) and UN Convention against Cybercrime (2024)
- Tallinn Manual 2.0 rule summaries (Cambridge University Press copyright — summaries only)
- UN GGE/OEWG reports and Paris Call for Trust and Security
- NATO Cyber Defence Policy and EU Cyber Diplomacy Toolbox
- US Executive Orders (EO 14028, EO 13800) and CISA Act 2018
- Wassenaar Arrangement Category 5 Part 2 export controls
- National cybersecurity strategies for 54 countries
- Attribution framework and cyber norms mapping tools
- Dual transport: stdio (npm) + Streamable HTTP (Docker)
